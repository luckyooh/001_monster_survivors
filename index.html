About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival action game where you must navigate through hordes of relentless monsters. As waves of enemies become increasingly difficult, you'll need to collect power-ups, upgrade your weapons, and develop strategies to survive as long as possible.

With its pixelated art style and addictive gameplay, Monster Survivors offers a challenging experience that will test your reflexes and tactical thinking. The game features various character classes, each with unique abilities and playstyles to master.

Play directly in your browser without any downloads or installations required. Challenge yourself to beat your high score and discover all the secrets hidden throughout the game!

How to Play

Controls

Use WASD or arrow keys to move your character around the map. Your character will automatically attack nearby enemies.

Power-ups

Collect gems from defeated enemies to level up. Each level-up allows you to choose a new power-up or upgrade existing abilities.

Survival

Avoid enemies and their attacks while maximizing your damage output. The longer you survive, the more challenging the game becomes.

Tips & Tricks

Focus on Movement

Keep moving to avoid being surrounded. Create space between you and the monsters to give yourself time to react.

Upgrade Strategically

Choose upgrades that complement each other. Building a focused build is often more effective than spreading across many different ability types.

Collect All Experience

Don't leave experience gems behind. Each level-up gives you more power to face tougher waves.

Learn Enemy Patterns

Different monster types have different attack patterns and speeds. Learning these will help you prioritize threats.
